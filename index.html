I’m an all-round product designer, covering UI, wireframes, prototypes, interactions, animations, icons, 3D, marketing assets, product direction, and more.

I’ve worked on dozens of projects of all sizes and budgets, from tiny startups you haven’t heard of, to giants like Rite Aid, Owners.com, and Verizon.

I specialise in iPhone apps but I’ve designed for Android, responsive web, iPad, watchOS, macOS, tvOS, and Android Wear. I’m a big fan of both the iOS HIG and Material Design. I do most of my work in Sketch, Principle, After Effects, and Xcode.
